GOOGLE DATA ANALYTICS CAPSTONE PROJECT

This capstone project is from the Google Data Analytics Certification Course offered by Coursera and accredited by Google. In this case study, I will be working as a data analyst for CYCLISTIC, a bike-share company. CYCLISTIC wants to get insights and recommendations on how to convert their Casual riders into Yearly Membership riders. The company is based in Chicago, USA and has over 5,800 bicycles that are geotracked and locked into a network of 692 stations across Chicago.
